Acacia look 🌍 concept board 001

from "tall young poplar" to "savanna umbrella" — four directions, same data model · generative sketches, reseed to see variants
· write-up in README.md · palette + strata inherited from mood-sketch 001

1 · Rising Umbrella silhouette — the core move

Short bole, low fork, angular limbs, and one flat pad per sector, parked just under its earned stratum ceiling. The flatten-at-ceiling rule already in grow.mjs becomes the hero feature: a level-up = the canopy visibly lifts. Asymmetric levels read as a natural multi-tier umbrella.

2 · Tiered Pagoda history layer — optional, on top of 1

Every stratum a sector has ever crossed keeps a thinner pad at that band — current level wears the fresh crown, older tiers dry to savanna gold below it. Blossoms sit on the tier rims where they already spawn. Gorgeous at 1–2 tiers, busy at 4×10 sectors → proposed as a toggle / replay-only effect.

3 · Savanna Night atmosphere — biome reframe, keeps the night garden

The four strata stop being rainforest fog layers and become sky bands over an open plain: ground haze (Novice) → dusk teal (Experimenter) → a thin gold afterglow (Practitioner) → stars only above the Expert line, so your tallest pad is the one thing silhouetted against actual stars. Grove-walk inherits scattered savanna neighbors readable at distance by pad heights alone.

4 · Thorn & Lace detail + material pass, after 1 & 3

Close-up character: zigzag elbow wood (the sap-light pulse running along it reads like a circuit trace), tiny non-glowing thorns at the kinks, foliage as thin lace layers of bipinnate leaflets glowing from within — and fireflies drifting in the shade under the umbrella, where savanna life gathers.

Recommended direction

1 + 3 together are the look — the silhouette and the world it stands in; each alone looks half-committed. 4 is the polish pass once the new skeleton exists; 2 stays an experiment behind a toggle.

Nothing in the data model moves: growth log, drivers, taxonomy, milestone gating, privacy and the replay all stay untouched. tree = grow(events, seed) stays pure — the tree just learns which species it is (algoVersion 2.0.0).
